Ikea is Life

23 February 2015

Do you ever just get the feeling you could live in Ikea? It just seems to have everything, and it’s like one big maze… And it doesn’t even stop at furniture! I might have gotten slightly too comfy on a random sofa at some point or other.
Ikea is one of the only places where shopping there can be called a fun day out. A whole day. In one shop. And at the end, you can even go for a meal, without even stepping outside! It’s weird and awesome… My room is basically just one big flatpack, I’m struggling to find any furniture not made by Ikea in it.
